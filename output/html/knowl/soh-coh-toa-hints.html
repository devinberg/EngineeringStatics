Hints.

  • Take care that your calculator is set in degrees mode for this course.

  • Always work with angles between 0° and 90° and use positive arguments for the inverse trig functions.

    Following this advice will avoid unwanted signs and incorrect directions caused because \(\frac{-a}{b} = \frac{a}{-b}\text{,}\) and \(\frac{a}{b} = \frac{-a}{-b}\) and the calculator can't distinguish between them.
