Three forces act on a machine part which is free to slide along a vertical, frictionless rod. Forces \(A\) and \(B\) have a magnitude of \(20~\text{ N} \) and force \(C\) has a magnitude of \(30~\text{ N} \text{.}\) Force \(B\) acts \(\alpha\) degrees from the horizontal, and force \(C\) acts at the same angle from the vertical.
